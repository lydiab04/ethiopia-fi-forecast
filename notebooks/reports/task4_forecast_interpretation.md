# Task 4: Forecasting Interpretation & Consortium Strategy

## 1. Projected Trajectories & Target Milestone
Under the **Base Scenario**, national Account Ownership is projected to cross the **60.0% consortium target benchmark by late 2026 / early 2027**, reaching **62.1% by 2027**. 

## 2. Key Event Drivers
* **Fayda ID Rollout & Telebirr/M-Pesa Interoperability:** Combined, these events provide the structural push required to move from 46% (2024) to >60% (2027).
* **Digital Payment Acceleration:** Digital payment usage is expected to scale rapidly, reaching **55.2%** under the Base Scenario by 2027.

## 3. Risk & Uncertainty Analysis
* **Pessimistic Risks:** Infrastructure bottlenecks or macro shocks could keep ownership stagnant around **54.2%** by 2027, missing the national target.
